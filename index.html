01 — The problem

Every night, people near you
are choosing where to eat.
Most of them never find you.

Not because the food isn't good. Because there's no system bringing them to your door.

People searching nearby don't find you

Someone in your area is deciding where to eat right now. They've never heard of you. They don't find you. They go somewhere else.

People who find you can't book

It's 10pm, they want to book for Saturday. There's no way to do it online. They call tomorrow — and by then they've booked somewhere else.

Deliveroo keeps 35% of every order

Every delivery costs you a third of the revenue. That same customer sitting at a table is worth double — and you pay no commission.

Happy customers don't come back

Someone has a great meal and leaves. You have no way to message them next week. Every new customer starts from zero.
