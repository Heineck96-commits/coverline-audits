01 — What I found

The credibility is there. The system to use it isn't.

541 reviews is exceptional — that's real customers, real quality, real trust. But someone who's never heard of La Gustosa has no way to find it, and no way to book when they do.

No online booking — phone only
Someone who finds La Gustosa at 10pm on a Sunday and wants to book for next weekend has to call. If you're closed, they can't. So they go to the next Italian restaurant that has a booking button. The quality that drove 541 reviews deserves a booking system that works at 10pm on a Sunday.
